About Vinayak Group

Chomu's Premier Educational Ecosystem Since 2004

Our Journey

Our Founding History

The Vinayak Group of Institutions began its journey in 2002 under the visionary leadership of Shri Navrang Choudhary. From humble beginnings, it has grown into Rajasthan's most comprehensive educational ecosystem, serving 32,000+ students across 18 institutions.

🏛️ Key Milestones

  • 🏆 2004: Vinayak PG College established (Flagship)
  • 👩 2007: Vinayak Girls College launched
  • 🌟 2013: Vinayak International College (NAAC A++)
  • ⚖️ 2014: Vinayak Law College (BCI Approved)

Vision & Mission

🌟 Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in education, nurturing future leaders who shape the world with knowledge, innovation, integrity, and physical fitness. Creating stress-free, job-ready professionals from rural Rajasthan.

🎯 Mission

Fostering academic excellence through rigorous coursework, innovative teaching, mandatory physical training (Yoga, Military, Scout & Guide), and holistic personality development. Transforming communities through value-based education.

💡 Philosophy

"Education is not just a degree—it's complete personality transformation." We reject the notion that fees alone purchase education. Parents and institution share responsibility for student success.
